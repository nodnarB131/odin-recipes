Lemon Pepper Wings Recipe

Home

Juicy, sweet, and sour chicken wings. Easy to cook up and great for parties!

Ingredients

    2 tablespoons of olive oil
    1 tablespoon lemon-pepper seasoning, divided
    1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    1/4 teaspoon paprika
    1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
    1 pound party chicken wings

Instructions

  1. In large bowl mix olive oil and seasonings
  2. Pat down chicken wings with paper towel
  3. Mix chicken wings in bowl
  4. Prehead airfrier/oven at 375 F
  5. Cook wings for 17 minutes
  6. Let wings sit for 5 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
