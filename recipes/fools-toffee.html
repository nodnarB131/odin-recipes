Fouls Toffee

Home

Delicious sweet but salty toffees that you and your family can enjoy during anytime of the year(especially during Christmas!).

Ingredients

    2 tablespoons of melted margarine or spray margarine
    2 sleeves of saltine crackers
    1 cup of butter
    1 cup of brown sugar
    1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
    1 1/2 cups peanut butter chips
    (Optional) Grated Nuts

Instructions

  1. Cover cookie sheet with parchment paper/foil
  2. Spray paper/foil with margarine
  3. Place a layer of saltine crackers on paper/foil to cover sheet
  4. Melt butter in a sauce pan
  5. Add brown sugar and boil hard for 3 minutes(stirring) until mixture comes to a froth
  6. Pour over crackers & bake for 5 minutes at 350 F degrees
  7. Remove from oven and sprinkle choc & peanut butter chips and let sit
  8. Spread melted chips to make a frosting over the crackers
  9. Put cookie sheet in freezer until hard enough to crack
  10. Enjoy!
